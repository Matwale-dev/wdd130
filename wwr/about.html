Wild Waters Rafting

Our Mission

Our mission is to connect people with nature through unforgettable rafting experiences. We believe in safety, fun, and respect for the river. Whether you're a first-timer or a seasoned rafter, we have the perfect adventure waiting for you.

History

Founded in 1998 by Risper Thuram, a passionate river enthusiast, Wild Waters Rafting started as a small family operation with two rafts and a dream. Over the past two decades, we've grown into a premier rafting outfitter, known for our expert guides and commitment to preserving the rivers we love. What began as weekend trips on the local river has expanded to multi-day expeditions and corporate team-building adventures. Our legacy is built on safety, adventure, and creating unforgettable memories for thousands of guests who return year after year to experience the thrill of white water.

Throughout our history, we've navigated challenging rapids, trained world-class guides, and developed a deep respect for the natural environments we explore. From the roaring class IV rapids to serene stretches perfect for families, Wild Waters Rafting continues to innovate while staying true to our roots: sharing the magic of the river with every adventurer who joins us. We take pride in our environmental stewardship, partnering with local conservation groups to protect the waterways we call home. Every rapid conquered and every river mile traveled adds to the rich story of our company, driven by a love for adventure and a dedication to excellence.
