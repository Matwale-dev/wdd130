Signature River Expeditions

Half-Day Rush

⏱ 3-4 hours | Class III-III+

Perfect for beginners and time-crunched adventurers. Navigate the famous "Roaring Falls" and "Whirlpool Corner" with a professional guide. Includes all gear, safety briefing, and riverside snacks. High energy, big smiles!

  • ✔ 8 miles of exciting whitewater
  • ✔ Small groups (max 8 per raft)

Full-Day Canyon Challenge

⏱ 7-8 hours | Class IV rapids + lunch

For those seeking a true wilderness immersion. Tackle continuous class IV drops, swim in emerald pools, and enjoy a riverside gourmet lunch. Our most popular trip among return guests. Advanced booking required.

  • ✔ 18 miles of untamed river
  • ✔ Complimentary wetsuit & splash jacket
  • ✔ Riverside BBQ lunch included
From $159 / person

3-Day Ultimate Wilderness

⏱ 3 days / 2 nights | Class III-IV & camping

The ultimate white water journey. Paddle through remote canyons, camp under starry skies, and experience untouched nature. Includes all camping gear, meals, permits, and professional guides. Life-changing adventure.

  • ✔ 40+ miles of river & side hikes
  • ✔ Hot meals & professional river kitchen
  • ✔ Geology & wildlife interpretation
From $499 / person
